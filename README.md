# ChiroNote — Cornerstone Health

A clinical documentation tool for chiropractic practices. Dictate patient notes by voice and generate formatted SOAP notes instantly using AI.

## How to Use

1. Open the link in **Google Chrome** on desktop (required for voice recognition)
2. Add a patient using the search bar or the **+ Add New Patient** button
3. Select your note type: New Patient SOAP, Follow Up, or Referral Letter
4. Click the microphone button and dictate your clinical findings
5. Click **Generate Note** to produce a formatted clinical note
6. Edit the note directly on screen if needed
7. Click **Save Note** to store it or **Copy to Clipboard** to paste elsewhere

## Spinal Segments

Click any vertebra on the right panel to mark areas of concern. Marked segments will be included automatically in the generated note.

## Requirements

- Google Chrome browser (desktop)
- Microphone access (allow when prompted)
- Internet connection

## Notes

- Patient records and visit history are saved locally in your browser
- Works best on desktop or laptop
- Do not use Brave, Safari, or Firefox — voice dictation requires Chrome
